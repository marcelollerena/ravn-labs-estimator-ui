# Estimator

## Description
An internal Ravn tool that accelerates project estimation by combining AI-generated first-pass estimates with senior engineer review. Upload a PRD, get a structured estimate in under 60 seconds, review and adjust with mandatory reasoning, and build Ravn's collective estimation intelligence over time.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Inconsistent Estimates
Different partners quote wildly different hours for the same project — no shared mental model, assumptions, or risk tolerances exist across the org. Estimator provides a standardized AI-generated baseline that every estimator starts from, creating a shared "Ravn way" of scoping work.

### Problem 2: No Institutional Learning
Estimates vs. actuals data is scattered across spreadsheets, Jira, and individual memory. Insights die after each project. Estimator captures every human adjustment with mandatory reasoning, tracks accuracy over time, and feeds corrections back into future estimates — knowledge stays in the system.

### Problem 3: Estimation Is Slow and Expensive
A proper estimate for a medium project takes 4-8 hours of senior engineer time — for an opportunity that might not close. Estimator generates a structured first-pass in under 60 seconds, letting the senior engineer focus on judgment, context, and risk rather than mechanical breakdown.

## Key Features
- Projects dashboard with status tracking across the estimation pipeline
- Draft auto-save and resume at the exact pipeline phase
- Active/Archived project management with search, filtering, and sorting
- PRD ingestion via file upload (PDF, DOCX, Markdown), text paste, or structured form
- AI extraction of project overview, features, constraints, ambiguities, and implicit requirements
- AI-driven first-pass estimation with per-feature complexity, hour ranges, and confidence levels
- Team recommendation with headcount, role mix, and seniority reasoning
- Risk assessment with likelihood, impact, and mitigation for each risk
- Comparable project retrieval via vector similarity search over past Ravn projects
- Human review with mandatory adjustment reasoning (the training data)
- Internal and client-ready estimate exports (PDF, Markdown, web link)
- Calibration dashboard tracking AI accuracy trends and human adjustment patterns
